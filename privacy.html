Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

At 2048 Play, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, usage, and protection.

What Information We Collect

2048 Play does NOT collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any identifiable data. We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to play the game.

High Score Storage

Your high score is stored locally in your browser using browser localStorage technology. This means:

  • Your high score is saved only on your device
  • No high scores are sent to our servers
  • No high scores are shared with third parties
  • You have full control over your high score data
  • Clearing your browser cache or using private mode will reset your high score

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies. 2048 Play does not store any tracking cookies on your device. We do not use third-party cookies for analytics, advertising, or user profiling. Your browsing activity is not tracked.

No Third-Party Services

2048 Play does not integrate with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data brokers. We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking tools. Your data is completely private and stays within the game.

No Advertising Networks

We do not partner with advertising networks or ad exchanges. The game is completely ad-free, and we do not share your data with advertisers for targeting or profiling purposes.

Game Performance & Functionality

The 2048 Play game runs entirely in your browser. The game code is executed locally on your device, not on remote servers. This means:

  • Your gameplay is not monitored
  • Your moves are not recorded
  • Your game sessions are private
  • The game can be played offline

Website Analytics

2048 Play does not collect analytics or usage statistics. We do not track page visits, user sessions, or device information through our website.

Your Rights & Data Control

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. However, you have complete control over any data stored locally:

  • Clear Your Data: You can delete your high score by clearing your browser's local storage or using your browser's "Clear Browsing Data" option.
  • Private Browsing: Use your browser's private/incognito mode if you want to play without any local storage.
  • Browser Settings: Manage your storage permissions through your browser settings.

Security

2048 Play website is served over HTTPS to ensure secure communication. However, since no sensitive data is collected or transmitted, there is minimal security risk.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at support@2048play.in. We are committed to addressing any privacy-related inquiries promptly.

Summary

In summary, 2048 Play is designed with privacy in mind:

  • ✓ No personal data collection
  • ✓ No cookies or tracking
  • ✓ No third-party analytics
  • ✓ High scores stored locally only
  • ✓ Complete user privacy and control
