What is 2048?

2048 is a captivating number puzzle game that challenges your strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. The game is played on a 4×4 grid where you merge numbered tiles to create higher values. Your ultimate goal is to combine tiles and reach the legendary 2048 tile. It's easy to learn but difficult to master, making it appealing to both casual players and puzzle enthusiasts.
